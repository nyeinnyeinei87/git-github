ELUX

ELUX first began right here in Asia in 1980. Michael Tien, from Hong Kong, started this clothing chain. Its speciality was based on producing quality career outfits for the working men and women. With a primary focus on the Asian market, this brand creates apparel for Asians. Today you can find it all over from United Arab Emirates to Thailand! It has also expanded to sub-labels from ELUX Black, U2 as well as At Twenty which are variations on the different kinds of outfits appropriate for the workplace. From formal wear Mondays to business casual Fridays, this is your go to place for the latest work wear! Invest in these apparels and straddle the line between fashionista and office-chic when you buy ELUX today!
